4.Secant Method

Step-1: Find points x0 and x1 such that x0

Step-2: find next value using
Formula-1 : x2=x0-f(x0)⋅ x1-x0/ f(x1)-f(x0)

or Formula-2 : x2= x0⋅f(x1)-x1⋅f(x0)/ f(x1)-f(x0)

or Formula-3 : x2=x1-f(x1)⋅ x1-x0/ f(x1)-f(x0)

(Using any of the formula, you will get same x2 value)

Step-3: If f(x2)=0 then x2 is an exact root, else x0=x1 and x1=x2

Step-4: Repeat steps 2 & 3 until f(xi)=0 or |f(xi)|≤Accuracy
